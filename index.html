    • WHY

      Brands are investing a lot of effort and resources to create interesting and credible content for their customers, while the most trusted content comes from the users themselves.

    • WHAT

      Therefore, brands should take the advantage from valuable content created by customers freely available on social media. Let the brands speak through the mouth of their customers.

    • HOW

      Use Native HUB. New narrative space for marketers. Aggregate customer’s content from all social media platforms, moderate, add quiz or lead catcher and publish into any digital channel.

    • Benefits

      Brand obtains ready, most trusted content created by customers.Take full control, no need to invest or take any risk. Easily publish, generate display, analyze reach, peaks, sentiment in real time. Use generated leads to drive remarketing.

    How it works

    Automatic method for for content aggregation, analysis and real-time display.

    • 1. Discover

      Discover content related to Your brand generated by customers. Browse, search and filter social network and RSS feeds.

    • 2. Aggregate

      Aggregate and archive information about the brand from multiple sources.

    • 3. Curate

      Create dedicated streams of information. Add native content - quizes, lead generators, polls etc. Manage content.

    • 4. Publish and monetize

      Publish in real time on any device: social media content and native ad formats

    • 5. Measure

      Analyze the results and check the reach of social media. Analytics, rankings, activity research. Respond immediately.

    • 6. Remarket

      Build wide network of reach with new Content ad inventtory through programmatic forms at Big Data scale.
